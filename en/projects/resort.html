Maldives · Coastal Resort Salt-Resistant Stone Facade Solution

🏝️ Project Type：Resort Renovation 🪨 Stone Types：Jerusalem Stone / Coral Stone / Travertine Noce ⚙️ Processing：CNCcutting / waterjet cutting / salt-spray protection ✨ Surface：matte / flamed / waterproof seal 📦 Lead Time：8-10 weeks 🔧 Installation：14 weeks 🏆 Landmark：No 🏅 Awards：—
Project Overview

Project Overview

The Maldives is one of the world's top island resort destinations, located in an extremely demanding tropical coastal environment. HSST provided a 15,000㎡ facade stone solution using Jerusalem Stone, Coral Stone, and Travertine Noce, with salt-spray resistant treatment and marine-grade sealant, ensuring the stone remains pristine under intense sunlight, high humidity, and sea salt corrosion.

Technical

Technical Highlights & Solutions

HSSTWith professional expertise and rich experience，we overcome challenges for every project。

🌊
Anti-corrosion Technology
Salt Spray ResistanceTreatment
Salt spray corrosion along the Maldives coastline is a fatal threat to ordinary stone. We used a multi-layer penetrating waterproof sealing system, forming a hydrophobic layer on the stone surface, effectively blocking salt spray penetration and extending service life by over 3 times.
⚓
Material Selection
Marine-grade Sealant
All facade stone joints use marine-grade silicone sealant, capable of withstanding seawater immersion and intense UV exposure, no replacement needed for 15 years, significantly reducing maintenance costs.
🏗️
StructuralProjects
15,000㎡External Wall Dry-hang System
Lightweight aluminum alloy keel dry-hanging system, total weight reduced by 40% compared to traditional steel systems. Each stone slab underwent mechanical calculations ensuring structural safety under extreme wind pressure (up to 250km/h).
