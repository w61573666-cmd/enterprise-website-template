Technical

Technical Highlights & Solutions

Faced with a massive 30,000 sqm hotel project, HENGSHENG MARBLE overcame multiple technical challenges with expert engineering.

📏
Challenge
30,000 sqm Large-Scale Flatness Control
Solution: Laser positioning + digital pre-layout system with flatness calibration every 500 sqm. All panels were pre-assembled and numbered at the factory, with strict on-site installation sequence. Final flatness achieved ±1.5mm, exceeding international standards.
🏛️
Challenge
200+ Custom Curved Column Stone Cladding
Solution: CNC 5-axis machining center for precision arc panel cutting, each piece verified with mold pressing. Curved columns use seamless dry-hang technology for perfect arc transitions with joint tolerance within ±0.5mm.
🎯
Challenge
VIP Area 3mm Extreme Tolerance
Solution: Individual laser measurement of every stone panel for presidential suites and VIP areas, creating digital twin models. Precision waterjet cutting + hand finishing, all joint tolerances controlled within 3mm — jewelry-grade precision.
