Technical

Technical Highlights & Solutions

Facing a mega hotel project of 10,000+ tons, HSST overcame multiple technical challenges with professional expertise.

📏
Challenge
Flatness Control for 30,000㎡ Super-Large Area
Solution: Laser positioning + digital pre-layout system, with flatness verification every 500㎡. All slabs were pre-assembled and numbered at the factory, and strictly laid on-site by number sequence. Achieved ±1.5mm flatness, far exceeding international standards.
🏛️
Challenge
200+ Irregular Column Stone Cladding
Solution: CNC 5-axis machining center precisely cut curved slabs, each verified by mold pressing. Column joints use seamless dry-hanging technology, achieving perfect curve transitions with joint tolerance within ±0.5mm.
🎯
Challenge
3mm Ultimate Tolerance in VIP Area
Solution: Laser measurement of every stone slab in the presidential suite and VIP areas, creating a digital twin model. Precision waterjet cutting + manual finishing, with all joint tolerances within 3mm, achieving jewelry-grade precision.
