Technical

Technical Highlights & Solutions

Large-scale commercial stone projects need to ensure aesthetics while meeting durability requirements for ultra-high foot traffic.

🏗️
Challenge
50,000㎡ Large Area Uniform Flatness
Solution: Using our self-developed "Commercial Floor Flatness Control System" combined with laser scanners for pre-construction floor inspection, developing optimal layout plans. All slabs pre-cut and numbered at the factory, ensuring no secondary processing needed on-site, reducing waste rate to under 2%.
🦯
Challenge
Slip Safety for 100,000+ Daily Foot Traffic
Solution: Professional anti-slip treatment (COF≥0.5) for all floor stones, with higher standard anti-slip processes at entrances, under canopies, and elevator halls. All treatments are internationally certified, ensuring pedestrian safety in both dry and wet conditions.
🏆
Challenge
10-Year Extended Quality Warranty Commitment
Solution: Complete digital project archives covering source traceability, processing technology, and installation records for each stone. Bi-annual stone maintenance service ensures the mall's stone remains in optimal condition throughout the 10-year warranty.
