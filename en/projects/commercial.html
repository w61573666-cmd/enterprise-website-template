Overview

HENGSHENG MARBLE provided a 50,000 sqm solution for premium commercial flooring and feature walls, using Crema Marfil, Grey Emperador, and Bianco Carrara with a 10-year quality warranty.

Technical

Technical Highlights & Solutions

Large commercial stone projects must ensure aesthetics while meeting durability requirements for extremely high foot traffic.

🏗️
Challenge
50,000 sqm Large-Scale Flatness Control
Solution: Proprietary commercial flooring flatness control system with laser scanning pre-detection. All panels CNC-cut and numbered at the factory, achieving <2% material waste rate.
🧯
Challenge
Anti-Slip Safety for 100,000+ Daily Visitors
Solution: Professional anti-slip treatment for all flooring (COF ≥0.5), with enhanced treatment at entrances and elevator halls. All treatments internationally certified for both dry and wet conditions.
🏆
Challenge
10-Year Extended Warranty Commitment
Solution: Complete project digital archive covering source traceability, processing techniques, and installation records. Bi-annual stone maintenance service ensuring optimal condition throughout the warranty period.
