Core Technology

Artisan Precision · Tech-Driven

5-Axis CNC Precision · Nano Sealing · Smart Quality Inspection

PRECISION MACHINING

5-Axis CNC Machining Center

Italian-imported 5-axis CNC equipment achieves millimeter-level precision for complex curved surfaces, 3D carving and custom profiling. From Roman columns to feature walls, every line is flawlessly precise.

NANO PROTECTION

Nano Sealing Protection

Patented nano-penetrating sealer bonds at the molecular level within stone micropores, effectively blocking oil, water stains and pigment penetration, extending stone life by over 50%.

SMART QUALITY INSPECTION

Smart Quality Inspection System

Proprietary AI vision inspection platform with 40+ test procedures scanning for defects at millimeter level, guaranteeing zero-error delivery of color, texture and dimensions.
