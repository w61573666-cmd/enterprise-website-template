Company Overview

Hengsheng Marble S&T Co. Limited

Hengsheng Marble S&T Co., Limited is a Hong Kong-based integrated premium natural stone enterprise serving the global market. Headquartered in the core commercial district of Mong Kok, Kowloon, the Group specializes in the direct quarry sourcing, precision processing, global trade, and custom engineering of premium natural stones — including marble, granite, luxury stone, travertine, and sandstone — providing end-to-end stone solutions from geological exploration to final installation for the global high-end construction market.

Since its founding, the Group has held fast to its mission of "Transforming Nature's Gifts into Eternal Architecture." Rejecting the traditional low-value model of the stone industry, Hengsheng integrates geological science, materials technology, architectural art, and sustainable development into a new paradigm. The Group positions itself as a global premium stone resource integrator, a creator of world-class architectural aesthetics, and a setter of international green building material standards — committed to becoming a century-spanning listed industrial group recognized in the global architectural canon.

The Group has spent more than 25 years building a global portfolio of premium natural stone resources, holding exclusive long-term rights to multiple high-quality primary quarries. We control top-tier production capacity of highly scarce stone varieties including G682 Golden Granite, white granite, natural marble, and volcanic stone — securing global pricing power and supply chain dominance for premium stone materials. Rejecting extensive extraction, we prioritize long-term ecological sustainability, strictly adhering to international ecological standards with a model of quantitative scientific mining coupled with simultaneous rehabilitation.

Across the industrial chain, Hengsheng has built a closed-loop system spanning "quarry exploration — raw block extraction — intelligent processing — global distribution — engineering installation." The Group operates international dust-free intelligent processing bases equipped with top-tier European imported fully-automated cutting, polishing, carving, and molding precision equipment, ensuring micron-level craftsmanship standards that maximize the natural beauty of every stone surface. Our service network covers global landmark projects including star-rated hotels, high-end commercial complexes, convention centers, national-level public infrastructure, and airport transportation hubs.

As a Hong Kong-registered international group enterprise, Hengsheng operates with a rigorous international financial framework, a global compliance operating system, and an international quality certification system. Guided by the transparent, prudent, and long-term business logic of a listed company, we reject short-term profit-chasing and focus on a century-spanning industrial strategy. The Group firmly believes that an enterprise's ultimate value is not measured by its scale, but by its long-term contribution to human civilization, urban development, and ecological protection.

Hengsheng Marble S&T does not define its height by industry scale alone. We define enterprise value by our contribution to human civilization, urban development, and ecological protection. Our vision is to become a world-class century-spanning listed industrial group — one that transcends borders and eras, is recorded in the global architectural canon, and leads the stone industry from extensive production toward a new era of high-end artistry, ecological sustainability, smart technology, and eternal symbiosis.

Core Strength

Core Strength

Eight dimensions building a multi-layered industrial moat

Eight Dimensions

Eight Core Strengths

Hengsheng's core strength is never a mere accumulation of equipment and capacity, but a multi-layered industrial moat built upon eight strategic dimensions — constructing an irreplaceable systemic competitive advantage from quarry source to global delivery.

01

Exclusive Global Quarry Rights

With over 25 years of dedication to the global premium stone resource map, the Group holds long-term exclusive rights to multiple high-quality primary quarries, strictly controlling raw stone extraction at source. We monopolize top-tier supply capacity of scarce varieties including G682 Golden Granite, white granite, and natural marble, securing global pricing power and supply chain dominance. All extraction plans strictly follow international ecological standards, implementing a sustainable quarry model of quantitative scientific mining with simultaneous rehabilitation.

02

Automated Intelligent Manufacturing

The Group has invested heavily to build an international dust-free intelligent mega-processing base, importing top-tier European fully-automated cutting machines, CNC precision machining centers, automatic polishing lines, precision carving machines, and special-shape molding equipment — with the entire process under computer-controlled precision operations. We have achieved a closed-loop production system from raw block slicing, fine grinding, shaping, to surface artistic treatment, maximizing the restoration of natural stone's original veining and luster with micron-level craftsmanship standards.

03

International R&D and Innovation

The Group has established an International Stone Art R&D Center, collaborating with world-renowned architectural designers, geological scientists, and materials engineers to continuously develop green eco-friendly stone processing techniques, weathering prevention treatments, anti-seepage technologies, anti-corrosion processes, and durability enhancement solutions — upgrading stone from ordinary building material to premium art architecture and sustainable ecological building material, driving industry-wide process standard evolution.

04

Integrated Global Supply Chain

With Hong Kong as the global operations hub, we have established warehousing, logistics, cross-border distribution, and overseas station service networks covering Asia-Pacific, Europe and the Americas, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia. Equipped with large-scale international exclusive warehousing parks and substantial ready-stock reserves, we ensure long-term stable supply for global landmark projects and cross-border infrastructure —不受地域、時差、產業波動限制 (unrestricted by geography, time zones, or industry cyclical fluctuations).

05

International Certifications & Quality Control

All product lines have passed international building material safety certifications across multiple countries, green ecological certifications, and international engineering quality standards. The Group has established a rigorous quality control system covering the entire chain — from quarry source, raw stone screening, processing production, finished product inspection, factory release, to transportation and installation. Every stone piece must undergo 40+ professional inspection procedures, eliminating defects, color variations, and fissures from entering the engineering pipeline — achieving zero-compromise quality with listed-enterprise-level internal control standards.

06

World-Class Engineering Excellence

The Group has long served global star-rated hotels, high-end commercial complexes, international convention venues, and national-level public infrastructure projects. We maintain professional installation and construction teams and project management teams proficient in global building standards, design aesthetics, and climatic environment conditions across different countries — capable of undertaking large-scale, complex special-shaped, and high-end artistic installation design and construction, with end-to-end capability from conceptual design and material selection to precision processing and after-sales service.

07

Listed Capital & Long-Term Industrial Strength

The Group's capital framework is robust and prudent, with sustained investment in quarry reserve expansion, R&D innovation, high-end talent development, and global client services. With sustainable development as our core goal, we focus on quality and reputation over the long term, ensuring persistent, stable, and high-standard service delivery to global partners — never pursuing short-term profit maximization at the expense of long-term value.

08

Eco-Tech Sustainability Leadership

We place human ecological and environmental protection at the foremost priority of industrial development. The entire factory operates with energy-saving, emission-reducing, dust-free, and low-noise environmental protection equipment. Waste materials are sorted for recycling and secondary utilization, and systematic vegetation restoration plans are implemented post-mining. The Group's pursuit is not unlimited capacity expansion, but the rational utilization of nature's gifts under the premise of protecting the Earth's ecological environment — preserving intact natural resources and a clean living environment for future generations.

Four Pillars

Four CSR Pillars

A truly world-class listed enterprise never proves its value through scale and profit alone, but defines its corporate soul through its responsibility to nature, society, humanity, and future generations.

🌿

Ecological Responsibility: Protecting Earth, Sustainable Symbiosis

Natural stone is a non-renewable gift from Earth, formed over hundreds of millions of years. In global quarry operations, the Group firmly adheres to the ecological philosophy of "simultaneous mining and rehabilitation," strictly implementing international environmental protection standards — reducing dust, noise, and waste emissions. We promote waste sorting and secondary utilization, and invest substantial resources in post-mining vegetation restoration and ecological rehabilitation — ensuring that the environmental impact of extraction activities is minimized, preserving intact green space for future generations.

🏛️

Humanitarian Responsibility: Building Human Settlement Civilization

We believe stone is not merely a building material, but a vessel that carries human habitation and civilization. Hengsheng consistently insists on serving global architectural projects with high-quality, high-safety, and high-aesthetics products, creating comfortable, durable, and artistic living and public spaces for people. At the same time, the Group actively participates in public welfare infrastructure projects, providing premium stone solutions for schools, hospitals, and public facilities in underdeveloped regions — using industrial strength to promote social equity and development.

⭐

Industry Responsibility: Leading Upgrades, Setting International Standards

As a leader in the global stone industry, Hengsheng continuously drives technological innovation and standard upgrades, leveraging its own R&D capabilities and processing expertise to lead the industry's transformation from extensive production toward refinement, intelligence, and greenification. We proactively share eco-friendly processes and quality control experience, promoting the establishment of ecological and quality standards for the global stone industry — guiding the entire sector toward a more sustainable future.

🤝

Employee Responsibility: Building a Warm, World-Class Team

The Group regards every employee as its most precious asset — providing an international training system, competitive compensation and benefits, and clear career development pathways, committed to creating a diverse, inclusive, and respectful workplace environment. We care not only about employees' professional growth, but also about their physical and mental health and family well-being, regularly organizing public welfare and team-building activities — enabling every member to realize their self-worth within the enterprise and grow together with the Group.

Hengsheng Marble S&T believes that enterprise value lies in contribution to society, reverence for nature, and service to human civilization. In the future, we will continue as an industry leader, shouldering responsibility for the Earth and humanity — driving the global stone industry toward a greener, more technological, and more humanistic new era.
