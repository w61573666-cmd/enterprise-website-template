Company Overview

HENGSHENG MARBLE S&T CO. LIMITED

HENGSHENG MARBLE S&T CO. LIMITED (Hengsheng Marble S&T Co., Limited) is a comprehensive high-end natural stone group enterprise headquartered in the core commercial district of Mongkok, Kowloon, Hong Kong, serving the global market. The group specializes in direct quarry sourcing, precision processing, global trading, and custom engineering services for premium natural marble, granite, luxury stone, travertine, sandstone, and other quality stones. We are committed to providing the global high-end architecture market with one-stop stone solutions spanning from quarry exploration to project installation.

Since our founding, the group has upheld our brand mission — "Building Eternal Architecture with Nature's Gifts" — breaking the traditional low-dimensional operational model of the stone industry and deeply integrating geological science, materials technology, architectural artistry, and sustainability. HENGSHENG MARBLE is positioned as a global integrator of natural luxury stone resources, creator of premium architectural aesthetics, and setter of international green building material standards, striving to become a century-lasting, publicly listed industrial group that transcends borders and eras.

Through decades of strategic investment in the world's premium mineral veins, the group holds exclusive rights to multiple high-quality native quarries, commanding top-tier production capacity for rare stone varieties including Golden Beige Granite (G682), White Granite, natural marble, and volcanic stone, while maintaining pricing power and supply leadership for premium stone globally. We reject extractive mining practices, prioritizing long-term ecological development and strictly adhering to international environmental standards. Our sustainable mining management model — quantitative scientific extraction with concurrent ecological restoration — embeds reverence for nature deep within our corporate DNA.

Across the industrial chain, HENGSHENG MARBLE has built a fully integrated closed-loop system: "Quarry Exploration — Raw Stone Extraction — Intelligent Processing — Global Distribution — Engineering Installation." The group operates international-grade dust-free intelligent processing bases equipped with European top-tier fully automatic cutting, polishing, and carving precision machinery, ensuring every stone meets micron-level craftsmanship standards. Our service network spans global landmark projects including luxury hotels, high-end residential developments, convention centers, national public infrastructure, and airport hubs.

As an international group enterprise registered in Hong Kong, HENGSHENG MARBLE S&T maintains rigorous global financial structures, worldwide compliance operations, and international quality certification systems, operating with the transparency, stability, and long-term vision of a publicly listed company. We reject short-term profit-seeking, focusing instead on a century-lasting industrial blueprint. The group firmly believes that a corporation's ultimate value lies not in its size, but in its enduring contribution to human civilization, urban development, and ecological preservation.

HENGSHENG MARBLE S&T measures its stature not by industry scale but by its contributions to human civilization, urban development, and ecological preservation. We aspire to become a premier century-lasting publicly listed industrial group — transcending borders, transcending eras, and inscribed in the annals of global architectural history — leading the worldwide stone industry from extractive production toward a new era of high-end artistry, green ecology, intelligent technology, and sustainable symbiosis.

Core Strengths

Core Strengths

Eight Dimensions Building Top-Tier Industrial Barriers

Eight Dimensions

Eight Core Strengths

HENGSHENG MARBLE S&T's core strength does not come from mere equipment and capacity accumulation, but from an eight-dimensional top-level industrial moat that comprehensively builds irreplaceable systemic competitive advantages — from quarry source to global delivery.

01

Exclusive Global Mineral Sources

The group has been deeply engaged in the world's premium natural mineral resources for over 25 years, holding long-term exclusive rights to multiple high-quality native quarries and strictly controlling the raw stone extraction source. We command production capacity for high-scarcity stone varieties including Golden Beige Granite (G682), White Granite, natural marble, and volcanic stone, maintaining global pricing power and supply leadership for premium stone. All extraction planning strictly follows international ecological standards, implementing quantitative scientific mining with concurrent restoration — a sustainable mining model.

02

Fully Automatic Top-Tier Intelligent Processing Capacity

The group has invested heavily to build an internationalized dust-free intelligent mega-production base, importing European top-tier fully automatic cutting machines, CNC machining centers, fully automatic polishing lines, precision carving machines, and custom-shape forming equipment — the entire process is computer numerically controlled with precision operation. We achieve enclosed one-stop production from raw blocks, slicing, fine grinding, shaping to surface art processing, maximizing the restoration of natural stone's original texture and luster with micron-level craftsmanship standards.

03

World-Class R&D and Craft Innovation

The group established an International Stone Art R&D Center, collaborating with world-renowned architectural designers, geological scientists, and materials engineers to continuously develop green eco-friendly stone craft, anti-weathering treatment, anti-penetration technology, anti-corrosion craftsmanship, and durability upgrade solutions. We drive the upgrading of stone from ordinary building materials to high-end artisanal building materials and sustainable ecological building materials, leading the entire industry's craftsmanship standards to continuously upgrade.

04

Global Integrated Cross-Border Supply Chain

With Hong Kong as the global operations hub, we have built a warehousing and logistics network covering Asia-Pacific, Europe & Americas, Middle East, and Southeast Asia — including comprehensive warehousing networks, cross-border distribution systems, and overseas service stations.With large-scale international exclusive warehousing parks and massive spot inventory, we ensure long-term stable supply for global top-tier landmark projects and cross-border infrastructure — unrestricted by geography, time zones, or industrial cyclical fluctuations.

05

International Authoritative Qualifications & Quality Control

All products pass international building material safety certifications from multiple countries, green ecological certifications, and international engineering quality standard testing. The group has established a strict quality control system covering the entire chain: from quarry source, raw stone screening, processing production, finished product testing, factory inspection & acceptance, to transportation and installation. Every stone slab must pass 40+ professional inspection procedures, eliminating defects, color variations, and cracks from entering the project phase — achieving zero quality compromise with the strict internal control standards of a listed enterprise.

06

Top-Tier Global Project Execution

The group has long been serving global star-rated hotels, luxury residential developments, international convention venues, and national-level public infrastructure projects worldwide. We have professional installation teams and project management teams who are well-versed in building standards, design aesthetics, and climatic conditions across different countries. We can undertake oversized volume, complex irregular shapes, and high-end art installation design and construction, with one-stop end-to-end capabilities from concept design, material selection, precision processing to post-installation service.

07

Listed Company Capital & Long-Term Industrial Strength

The group maintains a robust financial system with long-term investment in quarry reserve expansion, technology R&D innovation, high-end talent cultivation, and global client services. With sustainability as our core goal, we do not pursue short-term profit maximization but focus on long-term accumulation of quality and reputation, ensuring persistent, stable, and high-standard service commitments to global partners.

08

Eco-Tech & Sustainable Industry Capacity

Placing human ecological and environmental protection at the forefront of industrial development. All factories adopt energy-saving, emission-reducing, dust-free, low-noise eco-friendly equipment. Waste materials are sorted, recycled, and reused, and systematic vegetation restoration plans are implemented after quarry operations. The group's pursuit is not unlimited capacity expansion, but the reasonable use of nature's gifts while protecting the Earth's ecological environment —preserving intact natural resources and a clean living environment for future generations.
